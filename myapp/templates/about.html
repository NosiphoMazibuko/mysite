About The Rocking Stars

The Rocking Stars is a rock band formed in 2015, blending classic rock with modern energy. With a lineup of four incredibly talented musicians, the band has taken the world by storm with their unique sound and powerful lyrics.

Band Members

  • Lead Vocals: Jackson "Jax" Turner
  • Lead Guitar: Zoe Martinez
  • Bass Guitar: Marcus "Mack" O'Reilly
  • Drums: Emily "Em" Jacobs
